How SupplyVision Works

1. Ingredient Usage Tracking

Everything starts at the ingredient level — because that’s how restaurant owners think. Track ingredient use like daily rice consumption with realistic seasonality, weekend patterns, and random surges.

Our smart preprocessing pipeline cleans and structures this data for forecasting.

2. 7-Day Demand Forecast

SupplyVision automatically forecasts the next 7 days of need using historical data. Currently a simple moving average — but designed for smart upgrades like time-series LSTM forecasting.

3. Smart Vendor Matching

Knowing what you need isn’t enough — you need to know where to buy smartly. SupplyVision ranks vendor options by price and reliability — defined as consistent full, on-time orders.

4. Predict → Match → Deliver

We’re building a vendor portal where suppliers can match with predicted demand. And we're partnering with delivery services to automate the last mile from vendor to restaurant.

5. Event-Driven Adjustments

Graduation Day? Football game? Festivals? SupplyVision syncs with local calendars to help you plan inventory around big events — before the rush hits.

6. Ask the Chatbot

Owners shouldn’t have to dig through dashboards. Our AI-powered chatbot acts like a supply chain analyst, answering any inventory or forecast question instantly.
